Features

  • Sound Quality: Truly immerse yourself in your games with hardware-driven virtual 7.1 surround sound for precisely located audio. Inline Remote :Yes

  • Noise Cancellation: Redgear Cosmo 7.1 provides you a passive noise cancelling experience. Its closed earcups design blocks any kind of distraction from outside which helps you keep focus during the game for as long as you want
